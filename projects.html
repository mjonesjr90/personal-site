Trivium

Trivium is an intuitive and minimalist trivia app designed to test your knowledge. With access to thousands of questions, whether you want to practice for those trivia nights at your local bar or spend time earning badges within the app, there's something for everyone!

My Coffee Notebook

My Coffee Notebook is your digital coffee journal. You can track and rate your favorite beans and more. As you add your coffee, you will see them in your collection. Along with that, they are displayed in a map view based on their origin and where you purchased them.
