Malcom Jones is a driven leader, an innovative problem solver, a great collaborator, and passionate about technology and how it can change the way we live our everyday lives.

He currently works as a Product Manager on the Verizon Media Ad Platforms team working to develop features on the SSP, with a focus on Digital Out-of-Home. He has prior experience as a computer science professor, a technology architect, and solutions engineer.

Malcom received his B.S. in Computer Engineering and M.S. in Computer Science from Christopher Newport University.
