Malcom Jones is an innovative problem solver, a great collaborator, and passionate about technology and how it can change the way we live our everyday lives.

He currently works as an engineer with the AOL Platforms team working to help integrate mobile apps and websites onto the platform. He has prior experience teaching as a computer science professor, architecting technology strategy, establishing a strategic IT supplier relationship framework, and managing IT governance.

Malcom received his B.S. in Computer Engineering and M.S. in Computer Science from Christopher Newport University.
